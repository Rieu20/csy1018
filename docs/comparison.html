Job Comparison

This is a systematic and objective process used to compare jobs within an organisation to determine the relative value or worth of each job. There are various criteria's use in job comparison this can include educational background or qualification, skills required, work ethics, conditions and job responsibility.

The goal of a job comparison is to assign the relative parts of each job role and its financial value. For instance, the salary offered for that part of role.

The term Business Intelligence Analyst is used to describe someone who organise a business domain, documents the business system, assess the business model or its integration with technology to identify the needs and resolve business issues

    Experience in Data Analytics, Data Warehousing and Data Modelling

Web Games Developer

Web Game Developer is a software developer precisely known as video games designers specialise in creating web video games. Game developers may be involved in some numerous features of games creation from initial concept and story writing to the coding and programming. Additionally, possible areas of work for a game developer include audio, design, production and visual arts.

There are quite a few mechanisms involved in the evolution of a video game. Designers, producers and graphic artists all contribute to the final product. However, programmers and software developers turn the idea into code, which provides the game with its operational instructions. Game and software developers create the core features of a video game

Salary: £45-53,000 Yearly

    Creating story lines and character biography
